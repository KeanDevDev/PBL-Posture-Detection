What Is Good Posture?

Good posture is how you position and hold your body—whether you’re sitting, standing, or moving. Proper posture helps you move efficiently, reduces fatigue, and avoids putting unnecessary stress on your muscles and joints.

Benefits of Good Posture

  • Reduces back, neck, and shoulder pain
  • Boosts energy and reduces fatigue
  • Improves breathing and circulation
  • Supports bone and joint health
  • Improves confidence and appearance

Tips for Sitting Posture

  • Place both feet flat on the ground or a footrest
  • Keep your knees level with or slightly lower than your hips
  • Use the backrest to support your lower back fully
  • Relax your shoulders and keep your arms close to your body
  • Position your screen at eye level to avoid tilting your head

What Happens When You Start Detection?

Once detection begins, your camera will track your posture in real-time using body mapping technology. It will analyze your sitting position and provide alerts if improper posture is detected.
